Oh my, this blew my balls off!

ingredients:
 - Squid
 - Glutinous Rice
 - King Prawns
 - Ham

instructions:
 - Cook rice
 - Mash raw prawns and ham then mix with rice
 - Stuff into squid
 - Boil in teriyaki sauce
