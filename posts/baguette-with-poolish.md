## Ingredients

_Poolish_

- 500g White flour - 50%
- 500g Water (27℃) - 50%
- 0.4g Instant yeast - 0.04%

_Final Dough_

- 500g White flour - 50
- 220g Water (41℃) - 22%
- 22g Sea salt - 2.2%
- 3g Instant yeast - 0.3%

## Method 

- 17:00 Mix the poolish
- 07:00 Mix the final dough - fold two times
- 10:00 Shape into baguettes
- 11:00 Bake
